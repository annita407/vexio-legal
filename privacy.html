Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Vexio CreatorHub respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Vexio CreatorHub iOS application.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the platform.
  • Enable communication between users and influencers.
  • Display listings, reviews, and ratings.
  • Maintain platform security and prevent abuse.
  • Respond to support requests.

Payments

Vexio CreatorHub does not currently process or hold payments between users and influencers.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures and Firebase services to help protect user data.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion through the app settings.
