Vexio CreatorHub – Community Guidelines Last Updated: May 2026 Welcome to Vexio CreatorHub. Our platform is an iOS-exclusive space designed to help businesses, brands, entrepreneurs, agencies, and individuals discover, network, and connect directly with content creators and influencers across TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and X (Twitter). Vexio CreatorHub is a discovery, portfolio-showcasing, and networking platform. We are not a marketplace, we do not process payments, and we do not manage transactions or contract delivery. Because our users connect here but collaborate independently, maintaining a foundation of trust, safety, and professionalism is vital. These Community Guidelines outline the behaviors expected of everyone on Vexio CreatorHub. By accessing or using our platform, you agree to uphold these standards. Failure to do so may result in permanent account suspension. 1. Professionalism & Respectful Behavior Vexio CreatorHub is a professional B2B (Business-to-Business) and business-to-creator networking environment. All interactions must remain respectful, courteous, and professional. Professional Conduct: Treat all creators, brands, and agencies with respect. Communications should remain focused on professional collaborations, campaigns, and creative services. Discrimination & Hate Speech: We have zero tolerance for discrimination, hate speech, or harassment based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or age. Harassment & Bullying: Targeted abuse, stalking, intimidation, defamation, or demanding behavior is strictly prohibited. If a user asks you to stop messaging them, you must respect their request immediately. 2. Integrity, Authenticity & Fraud Prevention To maintain a reliable network for brands and creators alike, honesty and transparency are non-negotiable. Creator Authenticity: Creators must accurately represent themselves, their identity, and their work. Impersonating other creators, brands, or individuals will result in an immediate, permanent ban. Metrics & Engagement Integrity: You may not misrepresent your social media metrics, audience reach, or engagement rates. The use of fake followers, bought engagement, click farms, or automated bots to inflate your profile status on Vexio CreatorHub or your linked social channels is strictly prohibited. Portfolio Legitimacy: Creators must only display portfolio content, case studies, and campaign results that they personally created or heavily contributed to. Taking credit for work you did not produce constitutes fraud. Scams & Fraudulent Offers: Any attempt to defraud users, misrepresent services, pitch multi-level marketing (MLM) schemes, cryptocurrency scams, or bait-and-switch deals is strictly banned. 3. Communication & Anti-Spam Standards Our Direct Messaging (DM) features are built exclusively for initiating collaborations, discussing campaign details, and vetting professional fits. No Spamming: Do not send mass, unsolicited, or repetitive messages to users. Tailor your outreach to the specific creator or business you are contacting. Unrelated Solicitations: Do not use Vexio CreatorHub to sell third-party software, financial services, coaching programs, or products unrelated to content creation and influencer marketing campaigns. Phishing & Malicious Links: Do not send links to malicious websites, phishing pages, or malware. All external links shared in messages or profiles must be safe and relevant. 4. Prohibited Content Policies Users are prohibited from uploading, publishing, or sharing any content (within profiles, portfolios, pricing listings, or messages) that falls into the following categories: Adult & Explicit Content: Pornography, sexually explicit material, or highly suggestive content. Illegal Activities: Content promoting or facilitating illegal acts, drug trafficking, or the sale of regulated goods. Violence & Gore: Graphic violence, self-harm imagery, or threats of physical harm. Intellectual Property Infringement: Portfolios or profile assets must not violate copyrights, trademarks, or publicity rights of third parties. Ensure you have the right to showcase past campaign work publicly. 5. Platform Scope & Financial Responsibility Vexio CreatorHub is strictly a discovery and connection utility. To ensure clear expectations, all users must acknowledge the boundaries of our platform: No Payment Processing: Vexio CreatorHub does not process payments, act as an escrow agent, or provide billing features. Independent Transactions: Any financial agreements, contracts, negotiations, or transactions happen entirely outside of Vexio CreatorHub. Users assume 100% of the risk associated with payment, fulfillment, and contract enforcement. No Order or Delivery Tracking: We do not track campaign deliveries, drafts, or deadlines. We recommend utilizing formal contracts and external project management tools to protect your business agreements. No Peer Review System: Vexio CreatorHub does not feature a public rating or review system. Users are expected to perform their own due diligence, vetting, and portfolio verification before entering a business relationship. 6. Profile & Account Requirements Accurate Service Listings: Service descriptions, price comparison points, and base rates listed by creators must be realistic and reflective of their actual business standards. Single Account Policy: Users should maintain one distinct account per entity (one per creator/influencer, or one per brand/agency). Security: You are solely responsible for safeguarding your account credentials. Do not share your Vexio account login info with unverified third parties. 7. Safety, Reporting & User Controls We empower our community to self-moderate and protect their digital workspace through built-in iOS features. In-App Blocking: If another user is acting unprofessionally, sending spam, or making you uncomfortable, use our built-in Block feature to instantly sever communications and prevent them from viewing your profile. Reporting Misconduct: We encourage users to report profiles or messages that violate these guidelines. To report a user, tap the flags/report option within their profile or chat thread, or contact our support team directly. Investigation: Our safety team reviews all reports. While we cannot arbitrate contractual disputes regarding external work, we will aggressively enforce guidelines regarding platform behavior, spam, harassment, and fraud. 8. Enforcement, Suspension & Termination Vexio CreatorHub reserves the right to review user activity and determine violations at our sole discretion. Actions We May Take: Issue a formal warning regarding minor behavioral infractions. Temporarily hide or restrict a profile from search results and discovery views. Permanently suspend or terminate an account without notice for severe or repeated violations. Decisions made by the Vexio CreatorHub safety team regarding platform access are final. 9. Legal & App Store Compliance Vexio CreatorHub operates in strict compliance with the Apple App Store Review Guidelines (specifically Section 1.2 regarding User-Generated Content safety) and all applicable legal frameworks. UGC Safety: To remain on the iOS platform, Vexio CreatorHub enforces a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content or abusive users. We commit to reviewing reported violations within 24 hours and taking swift removal actions. Legal Compliance: Users may not use Vexio CreatorHub for any purpose that violates local, state, national, or international laws. Advertiser Disclosure Regulations: When entering into collaborations discovered through Vexio, both creators and brands are independently responsible for complying with local advertising laws, such as FTC disclosure guidelines (e.g., #ad, #sponsored) on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and X. Thank you for helping us maintain Vexio CreatorHub as a safe, highly collaborative, and elite professional network for the creator economy. If you have questions about these guidelines or need to report an incident, please contact us through the app support panel.
